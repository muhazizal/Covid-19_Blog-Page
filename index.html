About Covid-19

COVID-19 is a new illness that can affect your lungs and airways. Its caused by virus called coronavirus. It was discovered in December 2019 in Wuhan, Hubei, China.

How Covid-19 Spreads

The coronavirus - known as COVID-19 spreads from person to person in close proximity, similar to other respiratory illness, such as the flu.

Symtoms of Covid-19

The most common symptoms of coronavirus disease (COVID-19) are fever, tiredness, and dry cough. Most people (about 80%) recover from the disease without needing special treatment.

How to Protect From Covid-19

There is currently no vaccine to coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19). The best way to prevent illness is to avoid being exposed to this virus.
